## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Pxxl App LTD ('Pxxl', 'we', 'us', or 'our') collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use pxxl.app and its associated services.

By creating an account or using our platform, you enter into a contract with us and acknowledge that we process certain personal data as described in this policy. For processing activities that require consent (such as optional marketing communications), we will ask for your explicit agreement separately.

This policy is governed by the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR.

02

## Who We Are

Pxxl is operated by Pxxl App LTD, a technology company registered in Nigeria. For data protection purposes, Pxxl App LTD is the data controller of personal data collected through pxxl.app.

Data Protection Contact: Our designated data protection contact is Robinson Honour, reachable at privacy@pxxl.app. This contact handles all data protection enquiries, subject access requests, and breach reports.

Registered address and company registration details are available upon written request to support@pxxl.app.

03

## Data We Collect

Account Data: Name, email address, password (hashed, never stored in plain text), billing information, and GitHub account details where you connect your repository.

Project Data: We process your source code, build artifacts, and environment variables solely to build, deploy, and serve your applications. Environment variables — which may contain API keys, database credentials, and secrets — are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. They are not logged in plain text, not scanned for content, and are not accessible to Pxxl staff in plain text. Access to the encrypted store is restricted to automated build processes only, with access logs maintained for audit purposes.

Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and interaction patterns collected to operate and improve the platform. Where we use analytics, data is aggregated. We do not guarantee anonymization of all usage data — some records may be pseudonymous rather than fully anonymous.

Communications: Records of support requests and emails you send us.

04

## How We Use Data

Service Delivery: To provision deployments, manage your account, process payments, and provide customer support.

Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized access, abuse, and fraud. Security logs may retain IP addresses and request metadata for up to 90 days.

Platform Improvement: Aggregated and pseudonymous usage data helps us improve performance and reliability.

Communications: We send transactional emails (deployment status, billing, security alerts) as part of our contract with you. We only send marketing emails where you have given explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

05

## Legal Basis for Processing

We only process your personal data where we have a lawful basis. The grounds we rely on are:

Contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR / NDPA): Processing necessary to provide the services you signed up for — account management, deployments, billing, and support.

Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR / NDPA): Security monitoring, fraud prevention, and platform analytics. We have assessed that these interests do not override your fundamental rights.

Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR / NDPA): Optional marketing communications and non-essential cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time via your account settings or by emailing privacy@pxxl.app.

Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR / NDPA): Where we are required to process data to comply with Nigerian law, court orders, or regulatory requirements.

06

## Security Measures

We implement technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk of processing your data. These include TLS encryption for data in transit, AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data at rest, access controls limiting staff access to production systems, and audit logging of privileged access.

We do not claim that our systems are impenetrable. No security measure is absolute. We commit to implementing reasonable and industry-appropriate safeguards, not to guaranteeing perfect security.

Internal Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted on a need-to-know basis. Staff with legitimate access to infrastructure are subject to confidentiality obligations. Environment variables and secrets are not accessible to staff in plain text under normal operations.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@pxxl.app.

07

## Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

Service Providers: We share limited data with third-party providers who help us operate the platform (e.g., payment processors, cloud infrastructure providers). These providers are contractually bound to process data only on our instructions and to maintain appropriate security.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by a valid court order, law enforcement request, or regulatory obligation. We will notify you of such requests where legally permitted to do so.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

08

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your personal data: access (receive a copy of data we hold about you), rectification (correct inaccurate data), erasure (request deletion, subject to legal retention obligations), restriction (limit how we process your data), portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format), and objection (object to processing based on legitimate interests).

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@pxxl.app. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

Consent Withdrawal: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

Complaints: If you believe we have mishandled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) or, if you are in the EEA/UK, your local supervisory authority.

09

## Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. Specific retention periods are:

Account data: Retained for the duration of your account. Upon account deletion, personal data is removed from active systems within 30 days and from backups within 90 days.

Project data (source code, build artifacts, environment variables): Deleted within 30 days of project deletion or account termination.

Billing records: Retained for 7 years to comply with Nigerian financial regulations.

Security and access logs: Retained for up to 90 days, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

Backup systems may retain copies for up to 90 days beyond the deletion of active data. Third-party providers (e.g., payment processors) maintain their own retention schedules, which we cannot control but which are governed by their own privacy policies.

011

## Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a personal data breach, we will: (1) contain the incident and assess its scope within 24 hours of discovery; (2) notify the NDPC and, where applicable, the relevant EEA/UK supervisory authority within 72 hours where the breach poses a risk to individuals; (3) notify affected users without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in high risk to their rights and freedoms.

User notifications will include: the nature of the breach, categories and approximate number of individuals affected, likely consequences, and measures taken or proposed to address the breach.

To report a suspected security incident or vulnerability, contact security@pxxl.app immediately with the subject line 'Security Incident'.

## Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies required for the platform to function (authentication, session management). These are placed on the basis of contract and do not require consent.

We may use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our platform. These are placed only with your explicit consent, which you can manage via the cookie preferences banner or your browser settings.

We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.
