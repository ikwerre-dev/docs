# Best Firebase Alternative for Nigerian Developers in 2026

Firebase's real-time databases and authentication made backend development accessible to frontend developers, but Nigerian teams face Google billing friction and deep vendor lock-in. Pxxl offers a Firebase alternative approach where you control your stack.

## Firebase's Lock-in Problem for Nigerian Teams

Building on Firebase means using Firebase's proprietary query language, data model, authentication system, and hosting. When Google changes pricing — which it has done multiple times — Nigerian teams have no recourse but to pay more in USD or spend months migrating. The Firebase NoSQL data model also has scaling limitations that hit projects as they grow.

## The Pxxl Alternative: Open Databases and Full Control

Pxxl provides managed PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB databases — open standards you can query with any language and migrate away from freely. Deploy your backend (Express, Django, FastAPI) on Pxxl alongside your database and you have a Firebase-equivalent stack without the lock-in. Use any authentication library — Passport, JWT, or NextAuth — that works with your language of choice.

## Migrating Your Firebase App to an Open Stack

Start by exporting your Firestore data using Firebase's export tools. Map your Firestore collections to PostgreSQL or MongoDB collections on Pxxl. Migrate your Firebase Authentication users to your chosen auth solution. Deploy your new backend API on Pxxl and update your frontend to call your own API instead of Firebase SDKs. Most migrations complete in one to three weeks for typical Nigerian startup projects.
