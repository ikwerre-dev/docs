# How to Host Your Portfolio Website for Free as a Nigerian Developer

A live portfolio website is one of the most important assets a Nigerian developer can have when applying for jobs, freelancing, or building a personal brand. This guide shows you how to host your portfolio for free with a professional custom domain.

## Why Your Portfolio Must Be Always Online

Nigerian recruiters, international clients, and potential employers visit portfolio links at any hour. If your portfolio is hosted on a platform that sleeps idle apps, your site will take 30+ seconds to load — long enough for a visitor to give up. Pxxl's always-on hosting ensures your portfolio loads instantly every time, creating the professional first impression that can lead to your next contract or job offer.

## Best Portfolio Frameworks for Nigerian Developers

React with Vite or Create React App is popular among Nigerian developers for interactive portfolios. Next.js adds SEO benefits through server-side rendering, helping your portfolio rank in Google for searches like 'Nigerian web developer'. Astro is an excellent choice for static portfolio sites with minimal JavaScript. HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript also work perfectly on Pxxl and are often the fastest-loading option.

## Deploying Your Portfolio on Pxxl

Push your portfolio to a GitHub repository. Connect it to Pxxl and deploy — the whole process takes under five minutes. Your portfolio will be live at a \*.pxxl.app URL immediately. For a professional appearance, purchase a .com or .com.ng domain and connect it to Pxxl through the Domains settings. Add a meta description and og:image tag to your portfolio to improve how it appears when shared on LinkedIn, WhatsApp, and Twitter — essential for Nigerian developer networking.
