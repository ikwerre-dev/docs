# PlanetScale Alternatives for Nigerian Developers — MySQL Database Hosting

PlanetScale built one of the most developer-friendly MySQL experiences ever created, but its decision to remove its free tier and shift to enterprise pricing left thousands of African developers without an affordable managed MySQL solution.

## What Happened to PlanetScale's Free Tier

PlanetScale announced the removal of its Hobby plan in 2024, forcing free users to migrate to paid plans starting at $39/month — roughly ₦60,000 at current exchange rates. This price point is simply out of reach for most Nigerian developers, students, and early-stage startups building their first MySQL-backed applications.

## Pxxl's Managed MySQL for Nigerian Developers

Pxxl provides managed MySQL databases that can be provisioned in seconds from your project dashboard. Set up your database, copy the connection string into your application, and you're running. Pxxl handles backups, updates, and scaling — the same managed experience PlanetScale offered, at pricing that makes sense for the Nigerian market.

## Migrating from PlanetScale to Pxxl

Export your PlanetScale schema and data using mysqldump. Create a Pxxl MySQL database and import your export file. Update your application's DATABASE\_URL environment variable to point to your new Pxxl database. Test your application thoroughly and then deactivate your PlanetScale account. The entire migration typically completes in under two hours for most Nigerian projects.
