# Pxxl vs Namecheap: Best Domain Reseller API for Startups

An in-depth analysis and expert guide covering the best domain reselling services. We focus on low latency, Naira billing, Paystack support, and zero upfront setups.

## Overview of Pxxl and Namecheap Infrastructure

Pxxl and Namecheap both aim to provide domain registration services, but they target different workflows. Namecheap relies on older, heavier integration frameworks, whereas Pxxl offers a developer-first RESTful API designed for modern web applications.

## Wholesale Domain Pricing and Margin Management

Pricing is key for margins. Pxxl allows resellers to purchase .com.ng domains at ₦2,500 with zero upfront fees, whereas Namecheap often requires high monthly minimum commitments or charges higher rates for early-stage resellers.

## API Design, Documentation, and Sandbox Testing

Resellers need to build and test their integrations safely. Pxxl stands out by providing a fully functional sandbox environment with simulated endpoints, while Namecheap requires live transactions or complex test account setups.

## Payment Methods and Currency Support

For resellers in Ghana, accepting local payment methods is essential. Pxxl integrates Paystack and bank transfers natively. Namecheap operates primarily in USD, meaning resellers face high bank conversion fees and card decline issues.
