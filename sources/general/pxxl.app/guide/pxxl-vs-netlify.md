# Pxxl vs Netlify — Full Comparison for Nigerian Developers in 2026

Netlify built the modern static hosting category and remains a strong platform. Pxxl is the alternative built for Nigerian developers who want the same experience with better local economics and full-stack support.

## Static Site Hosting: Pxxl vs Netlify

Both platforms excel at static site hosting — connect your Git repository, configure your build command, and your site deploys automatically on every push. Netlify has a slight edge in build plugins and form handling for static sites. Pxxl has an advantage in full-stack support, letting you deploy a database-backed app on the same platform as your static frontend.

## The Full-Stack Gap in Netlify

Netlify's serverless functions have execution time limits and are stateless by design, which makes them unsuitable for long-running processes or database-heavy operations. Nigerian developers building full-stack applications typically end up combining Netlify with Heroku or Render for their backend — adding payment complexity. Pxxl handles the full stack in one place.

## Who Each Platform is For

Choose Netlify if you are building a pure static or JAMstack site with minimal backend requirements and your organisation has USD billing set up. Choose Pxxl if you are a Nigerian developer who wants a single platform for frontend, backend, and database hosting with pricing you can actually pay in Naira and support that understands the African market.
