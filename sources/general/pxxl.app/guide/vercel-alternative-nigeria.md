# Best Vercel Alternative for Nigerian Developers in 2026

Vercel is a powerful platform, but Nigerian developers face real barriers — dollar pricing, card declines, and US-centric infrastructure. Pxxl delivers the same instant Git deployments, preview URLs, and custom domains without the friction.

## Why Nigerian Developers Struggle with Vercel

Vercel charges in USD, exposing Nigerian developers to exchange rate volatility and Naira devaluation every billing cycle. Beyond pricing, many Nigerian Mastercard and Visa cards are declined on international platforms, making it hard to even create a paid account. Vercel's edge network is also concentrated in North America and Europe, which adds measurable latency for Nigerian end users.

## What Pxxl Offers as a Vercel Alternative

Pxxl provides automatic framework detection, Git-based deployments, preview URLs per branch, custom domains, and automatic HTTPS — everything Vercel offers — with infrastructure optimised for the African market. You can deploy Next.js, React, Vue, Nuxt, or any static site in seconds. The developer experience is identical to Vercel: push to Git, your app is live.

## How to Migrate from Vercel to Pxxl

Migrating takes under 30 minutes. Sign up at pxxl.app, connect your GitHub or GitLab repository, and Pxxl auto-detects your framework and build configuration.
