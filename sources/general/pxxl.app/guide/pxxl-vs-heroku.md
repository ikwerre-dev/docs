# Pxxl vs Heroku — Which Is Better for Nigerian Developers in 2026?

Heroku defined PaaS hosting for a generation of developers but its removal of free tiers and rising prices made it inaccessible to most Nigerian developers. Pxxl is the modern alternative that restores what made Heroku great.

## Free Tier: Pxxl vs Heroku

Heroku eliminated its free tier entirely in 2022. Its cheapest paid Eco Dynos cost $5/month per dyno in USD — and you need separate charges for Heroku Postgres. Pxxl maintains a genuine free tier for personal projects with no credit card required, allowing Nigerian students, bootcamp graduates, and independent developers to deploy real applications without any upfront cost.

## Developer Workflow: Pxxl vs Heroku

Both platforms use Git-based deployment and support environment variable management through a dashboard. Heroku has more add-ons available through its marketplace — logging services, monitoring tools, email providers. Pxxl's integrated approach means fewer third-party integrations but a simpler, more cohesive experience. For Nigerian developers who want to ship quickly without configuring many add-ons, Pxxl's simplicity is an asset.

## Performance and Uptime

Heroku's Eco Dynos sleep after 30 minutes of inactivity, causing up to 30-second cold starts for the next visitor. Pxxl's apps stay running on the free tier — no cold starts, no sleeping. For Nigerian developers whose apps need to be responsive the first time a client or recruiter visits, Pxxl's always-on behaviour is a meaningful advantage over Heroku's entry-level tier.
